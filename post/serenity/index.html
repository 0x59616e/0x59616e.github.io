Serenity

August 28, 2025

A melody percolates through my fading mind.

It is the melody that always echoes through the city when the darkness falls. Humans rush toward the sound, tossing bags into the truck that plays this melody.

As the melody approaches me, I gradually recall that I’m trapped in a bag, on something sticky.

A sudden jerk pulls me into the darkness, evoking lost memories that carry me back to what I thought was an adventure. An adventure into a mysterious cave.

——-

The entrance of the cave gaped like a dark mouth in the wall. Several of my friends had ventured inside, but none ever returned. Their absence left me afraid. But fear mixed with stronger curiosity. So I entered.

——-

The air stagnated, carrying a mixture of wood and steel scents. Below the ground of the cave I could smell the humans and cat. I placed my paws silently on the ground, each step careful as possible, praying the cat wouldn’t detect my presence.

Left paw forward, right paw forward, left paw forward, right paw forward…right paw forward…forward…

It took me a few seconds to realize that I was stuck.

My heart pounded fiercely, pumping blood into every muscle of my small body. My muscles strained, using every bit of strength to pull my paw free. My eyes filled with tears, knowing that the clock of my life was counting down. My consciousness faded, withering with every strain of my exhausted muscles.

I could only feel that I was thrown into a bag before losing my consciousness.

——-

The lively melody pulls me back to my dying reality.

Am I frightened of dying? No more hunger gnawing at my belly. No more cats chasing me to the edge. No more rain soaking my bones to the core.

But life gave me moments of sweetness too.

The moment I discovered a forgotten piece of cheese after days of gnawing hunger.

The moment I found a warm, hidden corner to sleep after narrowly escaping a cat’s claws.

The moment I felt sunshine warming my fur after endless days of bone-chilling rain.

How about you, my friend?

What makes your life worth living?

What memories will comfort you when it’s your time to go?

The melody gradually dissipates as my consciousness fades.

Beyond the pain of living, there is peace, I hope…
